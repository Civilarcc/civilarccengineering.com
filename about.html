About Us

Company Overview

Civilarcc Engineering & Project Consultant Pvt. Ltd. (CEPC), established on June 15, 2018, is a multidisciplinary consultancy firm led by a team of highly experienced and well-regarded professionals. Our expertise spans across Highways, Traffic & Transportation, Road Safety, Social and Environmental Engineering, as well as Urban Planning and Architecture.

We offer a comprehensive range of consultancy services, including feasibility studies, detailed project reports (DPRs), construction-ready designs, pre-bid engineering support, geotechnical investigations, engineering and social surveys, project management consultancy, supervision services, environmental and social impact assessments (EIA & SIA), design reviews, proof checking, road safety audits, and architectural and structural design solutions. We are also actively engaged in smart city planning and implementation.

Our innovative and cost-effective solutions have been well received by clients, helping us foster successful collaborations and build productive partnerships with various organizations.

Objective

We are committed to exceeding client expectations by delivering value-driven services that promote both economic growth and social equity. This commitment is upheld through the continuous improvement of our quality management systems, ensuring consistent excellence and long-term impact.

Mission

At CEPC, we believe that the foundation of a successful project lies in the seamless integration of design and construction requirements. By engaging clients and key service agencies early in the planning process, we ensure that our designs are practical, cost-efficient, and require minimal revisions.

This collaborative approach enables us to deliver innovative and tailored solutions that meet design expectations while staying within the defined budget and timeline.
